Key Problem: Overlap & Fragmentation

There are 10+ Vercel projects with heavy overlap. Payment handling exists in both tfc-pay (Stripe Checkout, 58 prices, MySQL) and square-notion-sync (Stripe Checkout, 4 tiers, Notion). Client dashboards span tfc-clients, portal/, and tfc-hub.jsx. Staff views are scattered across tfc-staff, tfc-mgmt, and fitclinic-portal.

The tfc-c / tfc-s reorganization consolidates all of this into two clear homes with shared backend services.
